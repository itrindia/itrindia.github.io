Frequently Asked Questions

What basic documents are required to start my filing? ▼

Typically, you only need to supply your primary identification details, Form 16, and consolidated bank statements. If you have active stock or real estate transactions, your realized capital gains ledger reports will also be requested inside the secure chat.

Can you efficiently handle global equity tracking and RSUs? ▼

Absolutely. We routinely manage specialized Foreign Asset filing schedules (Schedule FA) for tech professionals processing stocks vestings like RSUs, ESPPs, or options linked with overseas broker entities.

How fast will my ITR processing submission conclude? ▼

Standard wage configurations finalize seamlessly within 24 to 48 hours. Complex structural portfolios requiring multi-tier capital asset reconciliations complete over a few additional working check cycles.
